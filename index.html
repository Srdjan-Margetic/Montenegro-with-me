Around Montenegro

Spread at around 14,000 square kilometres and a population of 650,000, Montenegro is one of Europe's smallest countries. However the variety of landscapes Montenegro has to offer belies its physical size. Boasting one of the most dramatic stretches of coastline in all of Europe, as well as some of the continent's wildest and most beautiful mountains, tiny Montenegro is likely to confound most visitors expectations.

Fun facts

• Montenegro is the home to the highest Mausoleum in the world.
• Montenegro has a total of 117 beaches spread along the 294 km of the coastline.
